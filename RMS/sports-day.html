Sports Day Through the Years

Take a look back at some of our most exciting Sports Day moments from previous years:

Sports Day 2023

Date: January 20, 2023

The theme was "Unity in Diversity," with fun races, team sports, and a grand closing ceremony featuring a fireworks display.

Sports Day 2022

Date: January 18, 2022

Focused on eco-friendly initiatives, with relay races, a green pledge, and exciting soccer finals.

Sports Day 2021

Date: January 23, 2021

This year was all about inclusivity, featuring sports for differently-abled students and fun family races.

Sports Day 2020

Date: January 22, 2020

The highlight of the year was a marathon for charity, along with thrilling basketball and cricket matches.
